# Demo Scenarios

## Scenario 1 — Email Drafting

User:

Draft an email to John explaining that I cannot attend today's meeting.

Outcome:

Assistant creates a Gmail draft ready for review.

---

## Scenario 2 — Meeting Scheduling

User:

Schedule a meeting with Alex next Monday at 10am.

Outcome:

Assistant creates a Google Calendar event.

---

## Scenario 3 — Contact Lookup

User:

Find the contact details for Sarah.

Outcome:

Assistant retrieves the record from Google Sheets.

---

## Scenario 4 — CRM Update

User:

Update Rick's email address to rick@example.com.

Outcome:

Google Sheets record is updated automatically.
